About

I am a recently turned web-developer, looking to further develop my knowledge.

Whether it’s learning to paint, brewing my own beer or fixing vintage typewriters, I have always loved to make, fix and pick up new skills along the way. I love the process of learning and the satisfaction that comes from mastering a new craft.

For the last 4 and a half years, I have worked as a non-technical role for a tech start-up. I enjoyed working in a dynamic environment for a company that I passionate about. However, I wanted to get stuck into problem solving at a more hands on level and make better use of my strengths. I decided to learn coding so I could make the jump to development to make use of my technical abilities and creativity.

After completing General Assembly’s Web Development Immersive course, I was invited to join the instructional team for the next group of students as a Teaching Assistant. I am now looking for a position as a Junior Web-developer in supportive environment where I can continue to learn.
